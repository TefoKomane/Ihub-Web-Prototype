About iHub Africa

Our purpose is to unlock South African tech talent and connect it to opportunity.

iHub Africa is a free, full-time digital training initiative rooted in community, mentorship and impact. We help young people build technical skill, professional confidence and real-world experience.

What we do

We run a six-month, full-time web development programme that blends HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design and modern tooling with project work for local brands.

Alongside technical training, learners develop communication, self-leadership, teamwork and professional habits so they are ready for a tech workplace.

Our values

  • Access: removing barriers to tech learning for South African youth.
  • Agency: empowering learners to create with confidence.
  • Impact: teaching what employers need and communities value.

Vision

To build a future where South African young people lead digital transformation with purpose, creativity and integrity.

Mission

To provide free, high-quality training and mentorship that turns ambition into employability and impact.

Why iHub?

Because we combine practical skill-building with mentorship, community, and real-world project experience — not just lessons.
